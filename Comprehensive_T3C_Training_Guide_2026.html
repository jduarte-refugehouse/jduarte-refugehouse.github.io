Curriculum Guide & Content Details

Complete course descriptions for all 38 training courses in the Refuge House training library, plus the 6-module T3C Conversion series. Click any course header to expand details.

Pre-Service Foundation

Orientation — 4 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Pre-Service Only

Content: Introduction to Refuge House; philosophy, organizational structure, and policies; description of services and types of children served; type of business, leadership, mission, vision, motto, Board of Directors; organizational culture and goals; monitoring agencies; work groups; long and short-term goals; hours of operation; dress code; basic HR information; Religious Views; Compass Model; and Ezekiel 37.

Regulatory: RCC Contract • Agency Policy

Notes: Does not count toward the 35-hour pre-service minimum.

PRIDE Training — 12 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Pre-Service Only

Content: Constructive Guidance & Discipline of Children; Developmental Stages of Children; SIDS; Developmental Milestones; Attachments; Fostering Children's Self Esteem; Positive Interaction with Children; Strategies & Techniques for working with the population of children served; Supervision & Safety Practices in the care of children; Cultural Competency & Diversity; Different Roles of Caregivers; Measures to Prevent, Identify, Treat & Report Suspected Occurrences of child abuse, neglect & exploitation; Connecting with PRIDE; Teamwork towards permanency; attachments and losses; Strengthening family relationships; discipline; signs of sexual abuse; shaken baby; continuing family relationships; planning for change.

Regulatory: TAC §749.863 — counts toward the 35-hour pre-service requirement • RCC Contract

SAMA — Safe Physical Management — 16 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Pre-Service Only

Content: Assisting Process; Protection of Self & Others; Containment; Object Retrieval & Special Situations.

Regulatory: T3C Blueprint — behavioral management certification requirement • RCC Contract • Agency Policy

RH Trauma Informed Care — TBRI — 8 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Pre-Service Only

Content: Neurochemistry of Fear — Factors & Solutions; Empowered to Connect & Correcting Principles; Compassion Fatigue; Sensory Integration; Playful Interaction; Adverse Child Experiences (ACEs); Prevention & Management of Secondary Traumatic Stress; Trust Based Parenting.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • T3C Blueprint — evidence-informed treatment model • RCC Contract

Annual Renewal Required

SAMA Refresher — 8 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Assisting Process; Protection of Self & Others; Object Retrieval & Special Situations. Annual renewal of safe physical management certification.

Regulatory: T3C Blueprint • RCC Contract • Agency Policy

Notes: Counts toward the 30-hour annual training requirement.

RH Trauma Informed Care — TBRI Refresher — 4 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Annual overview of Neurochemistry of Fear — Factors of Fear & Solutions; Empowered to Connect & Correcting Principles; Compassion Fatigue; Sensory Integration; Adverse Child Experiences; Prevention & Management of Secondary Traumatic Stress & Trust Based Parenting.

Regulatory: T3C Blueprint — annual TBRI competency maintenance

DFPS Online Training — State-Mandated External Courses

DFPS Reporting Suspected Abuse or Neglect of a Child — 1 Hour

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Mandatory reporter obligations; definitions and thresholds for abuse and neglect; how to make a report to the Texas Abuse Hotline; documentation requirements; consequences of failure to report.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Medical Consent Training for Caregivers — 2.5 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Medical decision-making authority for children in foster care; types of medical consent; when caregiver consent is sufficient vs. when court authorization is needed; STAR Health plan overview; working with medical providers; documentation requirements.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Need for Normalcy Training — 2 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Prudent parent standards; definition of normalcy for children in foster care; age-appropriate activities and unsupervised activities; applying the reasonable and prudent parent standard; benefits of normal childhood activities to well-being, mental health, and developmental growth; caregiver and child responsibilities when participating in activities.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Psychotropic Medication for Children in Texas Foster Care — 2 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Overview of psychotropic medication use in foster care; consent requirements; caregiver monitoring responsibilities; working with prescribers; medication logs; what to report and to whom; child rights regarding medications.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Runaway Prevention — 1 Hour

Foster Parents Annual

Regulatory: DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Reporting Sexual Abuse Training — 2 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: Recognizing signs of sexual abuse; grooming behaviors; mandatory reporting obligations specific to sexual abuse; how to respond to a child's disclosure; avoiding re-traumatization; documentation; coordination with DFPS and law enforcement.

Regulatory: TAC §749 • DFPS Online Portal — state-mandated

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external)

DFPS Trauma-Informed Care — 2 Hours

Both Staff & Foster Parents Annual

Content: DFPS framework for trauma-informed care; how trauma affects child development and behavior; trauma-informed approaches in daily caregiving; alignment with TBRI principles; applying trauma awareness to placement decisions and transitions.

Regulatory: T3C Blueprint • DFPS Online Portal

Delivery: DFPS online training portal (external) • Complements Refuge House TBRI curriculum.
