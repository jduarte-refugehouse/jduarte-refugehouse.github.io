Mental & Behavioral Health Support Services CQI Framework

This CQI model is specifically designed for monitoring and improving therapeutic services for children with emotional, conduct, or behavioral disorders.
Builds upon Basic Foster Family Home CQI processes with enhanced therapeutic quality metrics and 90-day review cycles.

Program Director - MH Responsibilities

  • Oversee MH service package delivery
  • Monitor therapeutic service compliance
  • Review continued stay criteria every 90 days
  • Provide 15-day written confirmations
  • Lead MH-specific CQI reviews
  • Approve Logic Model modifications

Treatment Director - MH Specific

  • Supervise all licensed therapists
  • Review treatment effectiveness
  • Oversee behavioral interventions
  • Coordinate psychiatric services
  • Lead clinical team meetings
  • Approve step-down transitions

Licensed Therapists - MH Team

  • Provide weekly individual therapy
  • Conduct family therapy sessions
  • Lead group therapy programs
  • Document treatment progress
  • Collaborate on behavioral plans
  • Participate in 90-day reviews

Case Managers - MH Enhanced

  • Implement TBRI¬Æ in therapeutic context
  • Coordinate all MH services
  • Ensure therapy attendance
