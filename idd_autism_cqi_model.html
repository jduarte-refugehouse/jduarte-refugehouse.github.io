IDD/Autism Spectrum Disorder Support Services CQI Framework

This CQI model is specifically designed for monitoring and improving services for children with intellectual or developmental disabilities and autism.
Builds upon Basic Foster Family Home CQI processes with enhanced developmental tracking and specialized support metrics.

Program Director - IDD Responsibilities

  • Oversee IDD/Autism service package delivery
  • Monitor developmental service compliance
  • Review continued stay criteria every 90 days
  • Provide 15-day written confirmations
  • Lead IDD-specific CQI reviews
  • Approve Logic Model modifications

Treatment Director - IDD Specific

  • Supervise IDD-specialized therapists
  • Review developmental interventions
  • Oversee behavioral support plans
  • Coordinate with special education
  • Lead treatment team meetings
  • Approve transition plans

Registered Nurse - IDD Team

  • 24/7 consultation availability
  • Develop/update care plans
  • Train caregivers on medical needs
  • Oversee medication protocols
  • Monitor adaptive equipment
  • Coordinate medical services

Case Managers - IDD Enhanced

  • Implement TBRI¬Æ with IDD adaptations
  • Coordinate all IDD services
  • Ensure therapy attendance
  • Monitor developmental progress
  • Document life skills achievements
  • Support special education needs
