üèÜ T3C Basic Foster Family Home Enhancements

These enhancements apply to ALL foster homes under T3C, regardless of service package

16-Hour T3C Transition Training

Module 1: T3C Orientation & Vision (2 hours)
  • Understanding the T3C transformation
  • Benefits for children and families
  • What changes and what stays the same
Module 2: TBRI as Evidence-Informed Model (4 hours)
  • TBRI principles in T3C context
  • Connecting, Empowering, and Correcting principles
  • Documentation using TBRI framework
Module 3: Human Trafficking Prevention (2 hours)
  • Universal requirement for all caregivers
  • Recognition and prevention strategies
  • Reporting procedures
Module 4: Service Planning & Documentation (4 hours)
  • Foster parent role in service planning
  • Within 30 days requirement
  • Outcome tracking and documentation
Module 5: Enhanced Family Connections (2 hours)
  • Family engagement expectations
  • Supporting birth family relationships
  • Co-parenting strategies
Module 6: Add-On Services Overview (2 hours)
  • Kinship Support Services
  • Transition Services (14+)
  • Pregnant & Parenting Youth Support

Annual Requirements for ALL T3C Foster Parents

Universal Requirements: These apply to all foster parents regardless of service package

Annual Training Requirements

30 hours minimum annually

  • TBRI Refresher: 4-6 hours annually (required)
  • Human Trafficking Prevention: 2-3 hours every 2 years (required)
  • SAMA Refresher: 8 hours annually
  • Abuse/Neglect Reporting: 2 hours annually
  • Cultural Diversity: 2 hours annually
  • Additional required topics to reach 30 hours

Note: Service package-specific topics are integrated into the 30-hour requirement, not additional
